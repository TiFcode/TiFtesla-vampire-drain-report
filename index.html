Method

  • Only parked samples are used.
  • The selected daily time window is applied to every day.
  • Battery drain is estimated from battery percentage drops between consecutive parked samples.
  • Increases in battery percentage are ignored.
  • Gaps larger than 2 hours between consecutive samples are ignored for the drain sum.
  • Days with fewer than 2 samples in the selected window are excluded.
