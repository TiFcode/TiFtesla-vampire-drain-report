Method

  • Only parked samples were considered.
  • Only timestamps from 00:00 to 07:00 were included.
  • Battery drain is estimated from battery percentage drops between consecutive parked samples.
  • Increases in battery percentage were ignored.
  • Gaps larger than 2 hours between consecutive samples were ignored for the drain sum.
  • Rows are sorted by lowest temperature ascending.
